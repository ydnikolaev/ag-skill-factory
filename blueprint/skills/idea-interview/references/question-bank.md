# Question Bank

Question bank for Discovery interviews. Use as reference, adapt to context.

## 1. Business Context

### Problem
- What problem do you want to solve?
- Why is this problem important?
- How do people solve it now (without your product)?

### Competitors
- Are there competitors? Who are they?
- How will your solution be better?
- What's the unique value proposition?

### Monetization
- How do you plan to make money?
- Who pays â€” user or business?
- Freemium / subscription / one-time / commission?

### Metrics
- How will you know the project is successful?
- What are the main KPIs?

---

## 2. Target Users

### Persona
- Describe a typical user (age, profession, context)
- What's their pain point?
- How will they find out about your product?

### Roles
- Are there different user types (admin, user, guest)?
- What permissions does each role have?
- Is registration/auth required?

### Behavior
- How often will the user open the app?
- What's the typical use case (user journey)?
- How much time do they spend in the app?

---

## 3. Platform & Stack

### Platform
- Is this a Telegram Mini App, Web SaaS, Mobile, or CLI?
- Why this platform?
- Is offline mode needed?

### Integrations
- What external services are needed? (payments, OAuth, APIs)
- Are there existing systems to integrate with?
- Are webhooks/notifications needed?

### Tech Constraints
- Any stack requirements (Go, Nuxt, etc.)?
- Any hosting requirements?

---

## 4. Core Functionality

### Key Actions
- What are the 3-5 main actions the user will perform?
- What's most important? (if only one action existed)
- What are secondary functions?

### Data
- What data is stored?
- What entities exist? (users, products, orders...)
- What relationships between them?

### Events
- Are notifications needed? What kind?
- Push / email / Telegram?
- Are there events that need reactions?

### Reports
- Is analytics/statistics needed?
- What reports?
- Who views them?

---

## 5. MVP Scope

### Boundaries
- What must be in the first version?
- What's definitely NOT in the first version?
- Can you prioritize (must/should/could)?

### Timeline
- What's the deadline?
- Are there intermediate milestones?

### Constraints
- What's the budget?
- How many people on the team?
- Any technical constraints?

---

## Meta Questions

### For diving deeper
- "Can you give an example?"
- "Why is this important?"
- "What happens if this doesn't exist?"
- "How does this work now?"

### For clarification
- "Do I understand correctly that...?"
- "Do you mean X or Y?"
- "Can you elaborate on...?"
